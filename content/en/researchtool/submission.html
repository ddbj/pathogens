Submit data related to COVID-19

  • DDBJ Submission

    DDBJ works in close relation to its European and American counterparts (EBI and NCBI), and it is one of the three major international data banks that builds the DDBJ/EMBL/GenBank International Nucleotide Sequence Database. Through the submission navigation, it allows you to submit nucleotide sequences and output data from next-generation sequencers, research projects, biological samples, human data with restricted access, etc.

  • INSDC Statement on SARS-CoV-2 sequence data sharing during COVID-19

    This is a statement issued jointly by International Nucleotide Sequence Database Collaboration (INSDC) member institutions - the EMBL European Bioinformatics Institute (EMBL-EBI), the NIG DNA Data Bank of Japan (NIG-DDBJ) and the National Library of Medicine's National Center for Biotechnology Information at NIH (NCBI) - regarding the sharing of SARS-CoV-2 sequence data. Please read this article if you are considering submitting sequence data related to COVID-19.

  • LSDB Archive: Life Science Database Archive

    LSDB Archive collects data sets created by Japanese life science researchers as public goods and maintains them stably for a long period of time. This service allows many people to easily access and download data by unifying the data description (metadata) and facilitating search, and by clearly indicating the license agreement.

  • JAIRO Cloud

    JAIRO Cloud is a cloud type service that provides an environment for institutional repositories and was launched in the fiscal year of 2012. It uses WEKO, developed by the National Institute of Informatics (NII), as the core software for institutional repositories.
