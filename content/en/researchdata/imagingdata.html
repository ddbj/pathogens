Imaging data related to COVID-19

  • Imaging and Video Archive, National Institute of Infectious Diseases (NIID)

    This is an archive of images and video data related to the research in pathogens and infectious diseases provided by the National Institute of Infectious Diseases (NIID). It publishes the electron-micrographic images of the new coronavirus isolated at the NIID and the images of cytopathic effects of the virus. For the usage and specifications of the data, please refer to the description on the NIID website.
