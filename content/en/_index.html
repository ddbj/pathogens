The COVID-19 Data Portal JAPAN integrates and provides data and services to support research into the new coronavirus. This system uses the JAIRO Cloud infrastructure and is made available to the public in cooperation with the European COVID-19 Data Portal and with the support of many Japanese institutions. For information on the effects of new coronavirus on your life and its prevention, please refer to the special website set up by the Ministry of Health, Labour and Welfare.
